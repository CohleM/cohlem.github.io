LoReFT equation

ΦLoReFT​(h)=h+R⊤(Wh+b−Rh)

Analogy

  • hh: The original photo (pixels, colors, brightness).

  • RR: A filter that only adjusts “warmth” and “contrast” (ignores other settings).

  • Wh+bWh+b: The ideal warmth/contrast (e.g., “make it sunset-like”).

  • RhRh: Current warmth/contrast (e.g., too cold and flat).

  • Difference: “Increase warmth by +20, contrast by +15”.

  • R⊤R⊤: Applies those changes only to warmth/contrast (leaves sharpness unchanged).

  • New hh: A warmer, more vibrant photo!

Why it fits: LoReFT surgically edits specific features without altering the whole image.

Potential directions#

Steering for Robust Reasoning Chains using steering vectors to guide LLMs toward robust chain-of-thought reasoning, even when initial inputs are noisy or adversarially crafted or maybe.

Robustness Diagnostics Using Steering Vectors#

  • Objective: Use steering vectors as tools to diagnose robustness issues in LLMs, identifying weaknesses in specific tasks or contexts.

  • Approach: Design diagnostic steering vectors that amplify certain failure modes, enabling researchers to better understand and address vulnerabilities.
